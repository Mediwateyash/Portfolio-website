  • Online Pizza Delivery Website

    Developed an online pizza delivery website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL. The platform supports two types of users: customers and store managers. Customers can browse through various categories of pizzas, place orders, and interact with store managers through an integrated chat feature on the website. Store managers can view and manage incoming orders, accept or decline them, and communicate with customers directly.
